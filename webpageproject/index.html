Connectivity

Abbott's Gallant devices provide unrivalled remote monitoring potential, through the worlds first bluetooth based ICD. Patients can easily stay connected via smartphone app ensuring quick and semless data transmission to their preferred healtchare provider.

Physiological design

With its new physiological curve design, Gallant provides superior comfort and fit for your patients at implant. The curvature of the device naturally matches that of the patients chest for ease of positioning at implant leading to reduced device protrusion and pocket imflammation.

VF therapy assurance

Patients with fast unstable ventricular arrhythmias are more likely to experince failed defibrilltaion shocks due to degredation of arrhythmia while tachy detection occurs. VF therapy assurance counters this with an enhanced sensing algorithm designed to detect deteriorating arrrhythmias quickly and delivering treatment before they become irreversible.

40J delivered shock

Abbott's new physiological design comes with 40J delivered shock capabilities. This high level energy delivery ensures powerful reversion of life threatening tachy-arrhythmias. With Abbotts unique two tier capcitior system, charge times can be reduced resulting in shorter times from arrhythmia detection to delivery of life saving therapies

Sync AV+

Gallant CRT-D's have been enhanced with Abbott's Sync AV+ fusion algorithm designed to improve your heart failure patient outcomes, Sync AV+ looks to fuse Patients intrinsic right bundle activity with bi-ventricular pacing to produce a signficantly narrower QRSd compared with traditional bi-V pacing. Sync AV+'s new percentage based timing ensure a better physiologic function at varying heart rates.

Intuitive Programming

The Gallant platform keeps Abbott's recongised and intutitive programming outlay through its Merlin programmer platform, helping improve familiarity and ease of navigation when interrogating and optimizing your patients device.
